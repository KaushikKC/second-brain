# Generative AI — the basics

## In one line
AI that *creates* new content (text, images, audio, video, 3D) instead of just predicting a label.

## Key points
- Flow: data → model learns patterns → generates new content. Like an artist who studies 1000 paintings then makes their own.
- Spans modalities: text (ChatGPT), images (Midjourney/DALL·E), audio (voice cloning), video, 3D game assets.
- Key techniques: **diffusion models** (modern image generation), **GANs** (older approach), and prompting to steer output.
- Why it matters: generation is what made AI "explode" — the wave of content tools and startups.

## Why I saved this
One-glance reminder of what generative AI is and the main methods (diffusion vs GANs) behind it.
